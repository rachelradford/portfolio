Locaria

One of Forward's new companies, Locaria is a multi-lingual Search agency.

The website itself is available in 12 languages. However it was important for the team to communicate how they localise their services, rather than just translate.

The design accommodated itself for different cultures with colour and text sizes. I used large map imagery in the background to liven a text-heavy site and provide a local context.

More

This website was designed and built by me using paper and pencils, Windsor & Newton watercolours, Photoshop, TextMate and Middleman.
